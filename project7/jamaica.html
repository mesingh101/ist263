5-Day Itinerary

Relax at Seven Mile Beach, Negril, cliff diving at Rick’s Café, dinner with live reggae music.

Visit Dunn’s River Falls, rafting on the Martha Brae, explore local markets for crafts.

Day trip water horseback riding, hiking, and enjoy woodside jerk chicken.

Tour Bob Marley Museum, Devon House for dessert, evening at Port Royal for seafood.

Snorkeling at Montego Bay Marine Park and relax at Doctor's Cave Beach.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: November to mid-December
  • Currency: Jamaican Dollar (JMD)
  • Language: English, Jamaican Patois
  • Must-try foods: Jerk chicken, ackee and saltfish, beef patties
