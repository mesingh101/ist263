5-Day Itinerary

Jamaica is known for its vibrant culture, reggae music, and beautiful landscapes. Here’s a 5-day itinerary for your Jamaican getaway:

  • Day 1: Relax at Seven Mile Beach, Negril, cliff diving at Rick’s Café, dinner with live reggae music.
  • Day 2: Visit Dunn’s River Falls, rafting on the Martha Brae, explore local markets for crafts.
  • Day 3: Day trip water horseback riding, hiking, and enjoy woodside jerk chicken.
  • Day 4: Tour Bob Marley Museum, Devon House for dessert, evening at Port Royal for seafood.
  • Day 5: Snorkeling at Montego Bay Marine Park and relax at Doctor's Cave Beach.

Travel Tips

  • Best time to visit: November to mid-December
  • Currency: Jamaican Dollar (JMD)
  • Language: English, Jamaican Patois
  • Must-try foods: Jerk chicken, ackee and saltfish, beef patties
